AGASTYA

the latest hostel of VSSUT Burla

OVERVIEW

Welcome to Agastya, the newest addition to the vibrant campus life at VSSUT Burla! Nestled amidst the serene surroundings of Burla, Odisha, Agastya stands tall as a symbol of comfort, community, and convenience. Our state-of-the-art boys hostel is meticulously designed to cater to the diverse needs of our students, offering a harmonious blend of modern amenities and traditional warmth. With spacious and well-appointed rooms, high-speed internet connectivity, round-the-clock security, and recreational facilities, Agastya ensures a conducive environment for academic pursuits and personal growth. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing unparalleled support, fostering a sense of belonging, and creating unforgettable memories. Whether it's studying late into the night, bonding over shared experiences, or simply unwinding after a long day, Agastya is more than just a hostel it's a home away from home.
